Alaris Pump Preventive Maintenance & Inventory Reconciliation System

Built an Excel-based workflow to connect daily PM audits with a master inventory, reduce duplicate entries, flag missing assets, and keep monthly PM due counts accurate after completion updates.

6,000+Assets tracked

COUNTIFMissing asset logic
DailyOperational update
Case study details
  • Problem: daily PM audit entries had to match the master inventory, but duplicate and missing asset entries caused count mismatches.
  • Solution: created XLOOKUP-based PM date updates, duplicate asset highlighting, missing inventory alerts, and corrected backlog count logic.
  • Impact: improved audit visibility, reduced manual checking, and made PM status easier to track across a large biomedical fleet.
  • Tools: Microsoft Excel, XLOOKUP, COUNTIF, COUNTIFS, conditional formatting, dashboard-style reporting.
